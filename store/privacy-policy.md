# Stash — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 6, 2026

## What is Stash?

Stash is a Chrome extension that captures your X/Twitter bookmarks and builds an AI-powered knowledge base in your local Obsidian vault. It requires [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md) installed on your computer to view and manage the generated notes.

## Data Collection

### What we collect
- **Bookmark content:** When you bookmark a tweet on X/Twitter, Stash captures the tweet text, author name, handle, timestamp, media URLs, and engagement metrics.
- **AI analysis results:** Category, tags, topics, and summary generated by your chosen AI provider.
- **Q&A history:** Questions you ask and the AI-generated answers.

### What we do NOT collect
- We do not collect your browsing history
- We do not collect your X/Twitter credentials or session tokens
- We do not track which pages you visit
- We do not collect any personal information beyond what's in the tweets you bookmark
- We do not use analytics, tracking pixels, or fingerprinting

## Data Storage

- **All data is stored locally** on your device using Chrome's built-in storage (`chrome.storage.local`) and your local filesystem (Obsidian vault folder).
- **No data is sent to our servers.** Stash does not have servers. There is no backend, no database, no cloud storage.
- Your API keys are stored locally in `chrome.storage.sync` and are never transmitted to anyone other than your chosen AI provider.

## Third-Party Services

Stash connects to third-party AI services **only when you explicitly configure an API key** and enable AI tagging. The services are:

| Provider | Data sent | Purpose |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| Anthropic (Claude) | Tweet text only | Categorization, tagging, wiki compilation, Q&A |
| OpenAI | Tweet text only | Categorization, tagging, wiki compilation, Q&A |
| OpenRouter | Tweet text only | Categorization, tagging, wiki compilation, Q&A |

- Only the text of the bookmarked tweet is sent — no personal data, no credentials, no browsing history.
- You choose which provider to use (or none at all).
- These services have their own privacy policies: [Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy), [OpenAI](https://openai.com/privacy), [OpenRouter](https://openrouter.ai/privacy).

## Requirements

- **Obsidian** (https://obsidian.md) with **Obsidian CLI** installed. Stash writes Markdown files directly to a folder you select, which Obsidian reads as a vault.
- An **API key** from one of the supported AI providers (Claude, OpenAI, or OpenRouter) for categorization, wiki compilation, and Q&A.

## Permissions Explained

| Permission | Why it's needed |
|------------|----------------|
| `storage` | Save bookmarks and settings locally |
| `unlimitedStorage` | Support large bookmark collections |
| `tabs` | Open tweets in new tabs |
| `notifications` | Show brief confirmation when a bookmark is saved |
| `offscreen` | Read files from the Obsidian vault for wiki compilation |
| `sidePanel` | Display the Stash panel alongside your browser |
| Host: `x.com`, `twitter.com` | Detect when you bookmark a tweet and capture its content |
| Host: AI API domains | Send tweet text to your chosen AI provider |

## Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third party. The only external communication is between your browser and your chosen AI provider, using your own API key.

## Data Deletion

- Delete individual bookmarks from the Stash side panel.
- Use "Reset Everything" in Settings to delete all data.
- Uninstalling the extension removes all stored data from Chrome's storage.
- Files saved to your Obsidian vault remain on your filesystem and can be deleted manually.

## Children's Privacy

Stash is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.
